Fruit Rooms

Bring the whole family to Hungry Caterpillar Inn and experience rooms designed with your loved ones in mind! Our spacious family-friendly accommodations offer comfort, convenience, and thoughtful touches to ensure everyone—big and small—feels right at home. With plenty of room to relax, play, and enjoy quality time together, your family is sure to create memories that last a lifetime.

Our Fruit Rooms include:

  • Multiple Sleeping Options – Queen/king beds with pull-out sofas or cribs available.
  • Fully Stocked Mini-Fridge and Microwave – For convenient meal prep
  • Spacious Bathroom with Bathtub – Essential for young children.
  • Entertainment Options – Smart TV, Wi-Fi, and toys/games for kids.
  • Child Safety Features – Childproof locks, non-slip mats, and blackout curtains.
