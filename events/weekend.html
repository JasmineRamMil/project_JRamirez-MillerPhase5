Weekend Events!

We invite you to make your stay even more memorable by joining us for our special events here at Hungry Caterpillar Inn. From cozy evening gatherings to themed celebrations, each event is designed to bring our guests together for unforgettable moments filled with warmth, laughter, and charm. Join us to connect, relax, and experience the unique atmosphere that makes Hungry Caterpillar Inn truly special.

Friday:Experience an evening of enchantment at our Butterfly Orchestra Night!

Immerse yourself in the soothing sounds of butterfly-inspired music, performed live in the magical ambiance of our inn. Let the melodies take you on a journey through nature’s beauty and grace—perfect for a relaxing and unforgettable night. Join us and let the music flutter into your heart!


Saturday:Emprace Your inner Hungry Caterpillar

Step outside and join us for a delightful morning at our onsite Farmers Market! Explore fresh, locally-sourced produce, artisanal treats, and handcrafted goods, all set against the charming backdrop of our inn. Whether you’re here to shop, stroll, or simply savor the atmosphere, there’s something special waiting for everyone!

Sunday:Create your butterfly wings

“Unleash your creativity at our special Craft Night! Join us for an enchanting evening where you can design your very own butterfly wings. Whether you’re a seasoned artist or just looking for a fun activity, this hands-on experience is perfect for guests of all ages. Let your imagination take flight and take home a one-of-a-kind creation!”
